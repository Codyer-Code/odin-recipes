Nachos

Not many things in this world better than a steamy hot plate of nachos.

Hungry? Throw some chips, cheese, and a protein in your air fryer and 1 minute later you have some incredible nachos to fulfill your worldly desires!

Why are nachos so great asked no one ever? I'll tell you. Don't like beef, make it with chicken or beans or steak...you get it. Season it however you want. Make it as spicy as you'd like. Top it with the entirety of your fridge IT DOESN'T MATTER. Nachos are beautiful like that, just like you boo boo.

Ingredients

  • Chips, flavored with a hint of lime if you dare
  • 1/2 lb of ground beef (for the gains)
  • 1 bag of Mexican 4-Cheese shredded cheese
  • Veggies and seasonsings of your choice, im going no veggies as always cause it's cheap and easy

Steps

  1. Brown your beef and set it to the side.
  2. Place a piece of aluminum in the fryer to catch the drippy cheese.
  3. Place a layer of chips on top of the foil.
  4. Layer as follows: Chips, cheese, beef, cheese. Then season to your hearts desire.
  5. Repeat the layer process as many times as you need to fill yourself and friends!
  6. Air fry at 400 degree for 1-2 minutes depending on how crispy you want your chips
  7. Remove the foil and place on a plate for easy clean up, and enjoy!
