Spaghetti

Cleopatra herself did not look as beautiful as this plate of spaghetti!

Cheap and easy dinner options come together to create this beautiful dish you can enjoy any week of the night! Ingredients are cheap, prep time is minimal, and in 30 minutes you can feed a family of 5.

Spaghetti is great because it really does improve with how much time and effort you you want to send. You can go for jarlic or mince up fresh, basil is optional but will level up your dish, and DO NOT forget about the seasonings..a little extra just be sure never hurt anyone

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 16 oz pack of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of Spaghetti sauce
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 bell pepper of choice
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic

    • Seasonings

  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Itilian Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Turn on medium heat and place your beef in a saucepan to brown. As the meat cooks, break it into little pieces
  2. Fill up a pot with water and place it on the stove, on high, to boil water for the noodles.
  3. While the meat is browning and water boiling, prepare your veggies. Roughly dice up the onion and bell pepper.
  4. Once the meat is broken up a bit, season to your hearts desire.
  5. When the water finishes boiling, place your noodles in the pot twisting them as they cook to fit the noodles without breaking them
  6. Cook the noodles for about 10 minutes or until soft
  7. Once the meat finishes browning, add in your diced veggies, minced garlic, and season some more.
  8. Let everything cook together for a few minutes and then add your sauce, reducing heat to low and allow the mixture to simmer
  9. Once your noodles are finished, take out your handy colinder and drain the water. DO NOT RINSE!
  10. Enjoy! Meat sauce is done as well as noodles, plate up and be full my friend!
